Fruitsalad

Nutrition facts

Per Serving: 344 calories; protein 2.8g; carbohydrates 56g; fat 14.5g; sodium 25.8mg.

Ingredients

  • 2 bananas, cut into bite-size pieces
  • 2 apples, cut into bite-size pieces
  • 2 (15 ounce) cans mandarin oranges, drained
  • 1 (8 ounce) container frozen whipped topping (such as Cool WhipÂ®), thawed

Directions

  1. Mix bananas, apples, and mandarin orange segments together in a bowl; add whipped topping and stir gently to coat fruit with whipped topping.
